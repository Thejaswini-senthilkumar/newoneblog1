Celebrating The Ying and Yang Of Cinema



The blockbusters that made the whole world go black and pink in the summer of 2023. Oppenheimer, the biodrama with a dark theme alongside Barbie the luminating light hearted comedy. Cilian Murphy and Margaret Robbie just Nagasaki-eed the screens! BOOM! Barbie, wasn’t just Barbie, it was Ken's show too. The Kens stole our hearts just like they stole Barbie's dream house. Even a year after its release, Ken still lives in his 'Mojo Dojo Casa House' aka our hearts. Music was the element that pulled us all in for both Barbie and Oppenheimer. Dua Lipa made us levitating with her song 'Dance the night' and Billie eilish captivated our hearts with “What was I made for” Even Ken was surprised when he bagged the Critic’s choice award for his song “Im just Ken” . While on the other hand Ludwig made our hearts feel the gravity of Oppenheimer’s choices with--------“Can you hear the music”




The world witnessed a craze of moviegoers takeover when people were showing up in pink suits to watch Barbie adding to the glamour of this crescendo of contrasting modern cinema and displaying their passion towards the celebration of cinema

Barbenheimer has left an effect not just on Hollywood but on all of modern cinema.We saw how the world appreciated the two different factions of cinema while bringing together history and fantasy and showing the bittersweet truth behind the glorified lives of Barbie and Oppenheimer. Being Mass Cinema at its peak, Barbenheimer garnered massive critical acclaim with Barbie bagging 8 Oscar nominations and Oppenheimer 13. There is no debate about who lost.............ITS JAPAN!
